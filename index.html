How we met

I first met Tiffany in 2014, a few months after I moved to Raleigh. While out exploring the area, Tiffany and I crossed paths at North Hills Beach Music. I could tell she was eager to find a dance partner and I was happy to oblige. A week later we went on first official date at Crabtree Mall. We stopped by the Godiva store and got a bunch of chocolate (one of everything), and ended up finding a spot to sit and talk for hours. We quickly became inseparable and choose to spend any and all free time together. Before we knew it, the summer was over and we began our "long distance" relationship when she left for High Point University. After a year at High Point and maybe some coaxing from Dean to "Live the Tarheel Legacy", Tiffany made the choice to transfer to UNC Chapel Hill, ultimately bringing us even closer together. Shortly after celebrating her graduation, I proposed on June 26th, 2018. Of course she said Yes, and my life has never been the same.

Accommodations

There are many wonderful places to stay nearby, we recommend the following options.

Figure 8 Island Realty
For larger groups we highly recommend a rental home on the island. With the closest proximity to the venue and access to private beaches it's hard to go wrong. Wedding Rentals can be made for as little as 3 days. For accurate pricing please call (800) 279 - 6085.
Spring Hill Suites by Marriott
For smaller groups or shorter stays we recommend the Spring Hill Suites by Marriott. As one of the area’s newest hotels, it features modern spacious rooms with prime location located across from Mayfaire Town Center that offers great shopping and food options. A Room Block will be available (Information coming soon). Roughly 20 minute drive from the venue. A shuttle service will be provided the day of the wedding.
Blockade Runner
For those that want a private hotel room but still want to be on the beach, the Blockade Runner is a perfect choice. On the beach in the heart of the Wrightsville Beach area, it also has close proximity to nightlife and dining. Roughly 30 minute drive from the venue.
The Surf Suites
For those that want a little more room on the beach, The Surf Suites is a great choice. The Surf Suites were all just redone and are conveniently located next to Oceanic Resturaunt and Bar and a close walk to Wrightsville Beach nightlife. Roughly 30 minute drive from the venue.
